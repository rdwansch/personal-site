title: Why I Use Nuxt for Every New Project
date: 2026-06-01
description: Nuxt gives you file-based routing, SSR, and a great developer experience out of the box — here's why it's become my default.
tags: [Nuxt, Vue, Frontend]
---

Nuxt has become my default starting point for any new web project. The combination of file-based routing, server-side rendering, and a thoughtful module ecosystem means I spend less time configuring and more time building.

The thing I appreciate most is that it makes good decisions by default without being prescriptive. You can opt into SSR, static generation, or client-only rendering per page — and the mental model stays consistent throughout.

If you're building anything from a simple portfolio to a complex content-driven app, Nuxt is worth a serious look.
